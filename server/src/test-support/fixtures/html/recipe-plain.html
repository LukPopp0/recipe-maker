Golden Test Chili

A hearty test chili with plenty of visible text so the pipeline's minimum content pre-check passes comfortably, full of ground beef, pasta, and a rich tomato base simmered low and slow.

  • 400g ground beef
  • 200g lasagna sheets
  1. Brown the ground beef in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Boil the lasagna sheets until al dente and stir them in.
